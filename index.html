The gap

Your sensors generate data. Your manuals hold the answers. Your CMMS manages the work. But nothing connects them. Until now.

The approach

Ingest

Sensor data flows in continuously — any gateway, any protocol. Upload your API documentation and MaintIQ configures the connection automatically. OPC UA, REST, EtherNet/IP, AEMP.

Understand

AI learns what "normal" looks like for your specific equipment, at your specific site, under your specific operating conditions. Not static thresholds — living baselines from real data.

Explain

When something deviates, MaintIQ diagnoses why — citing the exact section in the manufacturer manual. Not a hunch. Not a threshold violation. A cited answer.

Act

Work order created in your CMMS with the diagnosis, priority, and recommended action pre-filled. Notifications sent. Zero manual steps from signal to action.

The difference

Traditional systems say "vibration exceeded threshold." MaintIQ says "bearing wear detected per OEM manual Section 4.2 — recommend inspection within 7 days. Work order created."
